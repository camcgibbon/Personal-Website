About Me

Hi! My name is Connor McGibbon. I am an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics.

Born in St.Paul, Minnesota, and raised in the city of Chapel Hill, North Carolina, I've been inspired by the rich academic environment around me. I often engaged with advanced Math coursework at UNC Chapel Hill. This early exposure helped develop my fascination for mathematics and algorithm analysis.

My passion lies in full-stack web development, creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between users and technology. Furthermore, I have a growing interest in machine learning, eager to explore its endless possibilities.

Goals

As I continue to hone my skills, I aspire to evolve into a proficient full-stack software engineer, specializing in building seamless, effective web applications. As a tech enthusiast and a dedicated student, I am always up for a challenge and ready to dive into the next big project.
